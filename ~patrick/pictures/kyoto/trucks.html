Loud Trucks

This type of van is pretty common in Kyoto. It's got loud speakers attached to the roof. I think these vans are used for people running for public office or by people that want the emperor to come back to Kyoto from Tokyo. I'm told that getting the emperor back to Kyoto is a very popular theme of by these people with these vans. I think this one was someone running for public office. This one was a loop of some people talking, but there are some in my neighborhood that drive around playing what I'm told are Japanese war songs. They rock!
