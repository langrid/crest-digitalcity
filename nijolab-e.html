Public Transportation Time Tables

  • Kyoto station -> Kyoto shiyakusyo-mae
    • Subway Karasuma line for Kokusai kaikan
      Change to Tozai line for Goryo, Daigo at Karasuma oike station
    • Bus route #4 for Matsugasaki, Midorogaike
    • Bus route #17 for Kawaramachi, Shinrinshako
    • Bus route #205 for Shijo kawaramachi, Kitaoji bus terminal

  • Kyoto shiyakusyo-mae -> Kyoto station
    • Subway Tozai line for Karasuma oike, Nijo
      Change to Karasuma line for Takeda, Shintanabe, and Kintetsu Nara at Karasuma oike station
    • Bus route #4 for Kyoto station
    • Bus route #17 for Kyoto station
    • Bus route #205 for Kyoto station
