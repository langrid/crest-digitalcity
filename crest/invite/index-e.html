Invited Researcher

Invited Researcher：should apply his/her application to JST, following the process.
    JST supports his/her airfare to Japan, domestic travel fee, and allowances.

1.Researcher for short term stay：within total 7 days (from arriving to leaving day)
    Application
    Deadline：One month before your arrive
    Send your：CV and travel schedule
    ↓
    Invitation letter arrives．
    ↓
    Book your flight.
    Fax a completed Reimbursement Form and your ticket reciept to Digital City Research Center.
    Also let us know your final travel schedule and accomodation info.
    ↓
    Welcome!
    *Please bring a reciept of your ticket purchase with you.
    ↓
    All payments by JST paid in Japanese Yen.
    *Reimbursement in Japanese Yen according to TTS Rate of your purchase date.
2.Researcher for long term stay：over 7 days (from arriving to leaving day)
    Application
    Deadline：Two months before your arrive
    Send your：CV with date and signature, a face picture (3.5x4.5cm), a copy of passport (visa), and travel schedule.
    ↓
    Invitation letter arrives．
    ↓
    Book your flight.
    Fax a completed Reimbursement Form and your ticket reciept to Digital City Research Center.
    Also let us know your final travel schedule and accomodation info.
    ↓
    Welcome to Kyoto, Japan!
    *Please bring a reciept of your ticket purchase with you.
    ↓
    All payments by JST paid in Japanese Yen.
    *Reimbursement in Japanese Yen according to TTS Rate of your purchase date.
