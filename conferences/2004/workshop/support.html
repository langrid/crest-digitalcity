Travel Support
We have closed applications of travel grant. See you in Kyoto.
We provide limited financial supports to students and other researchers whose papers are accepted to give their presentations at the workshop.

Travel support will consist of round-trip airfare at the discount economy rate from your nearest airport to Kansai Airport(KIX). Return flight from any airports other than KIX will not be available through travel support provided by the meeting.

If total requests to travel support are more than our budget, we may not be able to cover your travel.
In order to apply for travel grant please send the following information to workshop@digitalcity.jst.go.jp by OCTOBER 10th.

Your Name
Detailed address and affiliation
Title of accepted paper
Estimated airfare cost for a discounted economy ticket
