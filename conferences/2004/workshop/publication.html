Instruction for Publication

In the preparation of your manuscript, please follow the instructions available at the Springer's site.
http://www.springeronline.com/sgw/cda/frontpage/0,11855,5-164-2-72376-0,00.html

Length: The length is limited to 15 pages and should be longer than 12 pages. Please shorten your abstract to 150 words.

Contact Info: Your manuscript should include a complete affiliation, a surface mail address and an email address for all contributing authors.

Format: In the title, the first letter of each main word should be capitalized. Please be sure that there is no last line of a paragraph at the top of a page. Section and subsection heads should remain with at least 2 lines of body text when near the end of a page.

Printing: Please be sure your manuscript is formatted for A4 paper size (210mm x 297mm). We recommend images to be at least 300 or 600 dpi for quality reproduction. Please remember that the figures will be printed in grayscale.

Copyright form: The copyright form can be obtained from:
http://www.springeronline.com/sgw/cda/pageitems/document/cda_downloaddocument/0,11996,0-0-45-72914-0,00.pdf
 Please fill in the form and give it to us when you come to the workshop.
