Access from Kyoto Station
  1. Take the JR San-in Line to "Tanbaguchi Station". From there, walk west for 5 minutes.
  2. Take City Bus 73 or 75. Get off at the "Kyoto Research Park Mae" stop.
Access to Kyoto Station from KIX
  1. Take JR (Japan Railway) Kansai Airport Express "Haruka" to Kyoto.
    The train leaves every half an hour.
    It takes 73 minutes to Kyoto station and costs 3,490 yen.
  2. Alternatively, take the airport limousine bus service to Kyoto station.
    The bus leaves every hour on the hour.
    It takes two hours or more and costs 2,300 yen. (less than "Haruka")

For more information about Haruka service, please refer to the JR West Haruka Page or Kansai Airport Home Page.
